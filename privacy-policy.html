Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

At Pet Finder Helper, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our pet finding application and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Address and location data
  • Pet information (name, species, breed, photos)
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information (if applicable)

Location Data

We collect location data to:

  • Track your pet's location when lost
  • Show nearby pet shelters and veterinary clinics
  • Connect you with local pet finders

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your pet finding requests
  • Send you important notifications
  • Improve our app functionality
  • Communicate with you about our services

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Pet shelters and veterinary clinics
  • Local pet finders and volunteers
  • Service providers who assist our operations
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Access controls and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
