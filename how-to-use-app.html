How to Use Pet Finder Helper

Follow these simple steps to make the most of our pet finding application.

Getting Started

  • Download the app from the Google Play Store
  • Create your account using your email or phone number
  • Complete your profile with your contact information
  • Add your pet's information to your profile

Adding Your Pet

  • Click on the "Add Pet" button in the main menu
  • Fill in your pet's details (name, species, breed, age)
  • Upload clear photos of your pet from different angles
  • Add any distinguishing features or marks
  • Save your pet's information

Important Tips

  • Use clear, well-lit photos of your pet
  • Include photos showing any unique markings
  • Keep your contact information up to date
  • Enable location services for better tracking

Reporting a Lost Pet

  • Open the app and go to the "Report Lost Pet" section
  • Select your pet from your profile
  • Enter the last known location
  • Add any additional details about when and where your pet was last seen
  • Submit the report to notify the community

Finding a Lost Pet

  • Use the search function to look for pets in your area
  • Filter results by species, breed, or location
  • Contact the pet owner directly through the app
  • Share found pet information with your local community

Using Location Tracking

Our app uses GPS technology to help track your pet's location:

  • Enable location services in your device settings
  • Allow the app to access location when prompted
  • View your pet's location history in the tracking section
  • Set up geofencing alerts for your home area

Community Features

  • Join local pet finder groups
  • Share updates about lost pets in your area
  • Connect with local shelters and veterinarians
  • Participate in community search efforts
